What payment methods do you accept? Payment options for your convenience.

We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for your convenience.

 How can I track my order? Stay updated on your order status.

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number and a link to track your shipment.

 What is your return policy? Understanding our return process.

We offer a 30-day return policy on most items. If you're not satisfied, you can return your product for a full refund, provided it is in its original condition.

 Do you ship internationally? Availability of international shipping.

Yes, we ship to many countries worldwide. Shipping fees and delivery times vary based on the destination.

How can I contact customer support? Get assistance when you need it.

You can reach our customer support team via email at mirabelle-shop@outlook.de or by using the contact form on our website.

 What should I do if I receive a damaged item? Steps to take if your order is not as expected.

If you receive a damaged item, please contact our customer support team within 48 hours, and we will assist you in resolving the issue.

 Can I change my order after it has been placed? Modifying orders once they're submitted.

Unfortunately, once an order is placed, we cannot modify it. However, you can cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it and reorder.

 How do I know if an item is in stock? Checking product availability.

Our website is updated in real-time. If an item is available for purchase, it is in stock.

 What happens if my order is lost in transit? Resolving shipping issues.

If your order does not arrive within the estimated delivery time, please contact us, and we will investigate the issue with the carrier.

 How often do you restock items? Understanding our inventory management.

We typically restock items based on demand. If an item is out of stock, you can sign up for notifications to be informed when it becomes available again.

 What is your privacy policy? Your information is safe with us.

We take your privacy seriously. Please review our Privacy Policy page for details on how we collect, use, and protect your information.

 Can I use multiple discount codes on one order? Clarifying discount code usage.

Unfortunately, only one discount code can be applied per order. Make sure to use the one that gives you the best savings!

 How do I unsubscribe from your newsletter? Managing your email preferences.

You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the “unsubscribe” link at the bottom of our newsletters.

 How can I change my account information? Updating your profile easily.

Log in to your account, go to the "Account Settings" section, and you can update your information there.
